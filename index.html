Types of Medical Imaging

There are several types of medical imaging. Some of the types of medical imaging include X-ray, Cat Scan, Magnetic Resonance Imaging, and Nuclear Medicine. Each type of medical imaging uses a unique way of creating an image. Some medical imaging involves radiation, sound waves, and magnetic resonance or frequencies.

In order to understand which type of imaging is necessary, a doctor must understand how each one works and what type of image is created. For example, Cat Scan and X-ray use radiation to create an image of bones or vasculature. Ultrasound uses sound waves to create an image and is used frequently for pregnant women because there is no radiation involved. Magnetic Resonance imaging uses magnetic resonance and frequencies from the body to create an image of great detail and will show muscle, tissue, bone and tumors.
